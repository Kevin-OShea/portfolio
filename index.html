My love for computer programming started with my good friend entering us into a Game Jam, an event that challenges game designers to create the best game they can within 3 day's. Over that time period, I developed a love for logical complexities and the eloquent solutions that can come from coding. After that experience, I decided to go forward with a renewed vigor into college with computer science in mind.
